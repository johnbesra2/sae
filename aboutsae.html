About



Scuderia IITH RACING

IITH Racing is a team of Engineering students from IIT Hyderabad who shares a passion for Automobiles and work into designing, building and testing an All-Terrain Car. Making ATV better and team stronger every year. This team was founded in year 2013 and since then, we have been participating in BAJA SAE India and competing against teams from all over India.



BAJA SAE

BAJA SAE is a student competition that involves designing, Engineering , Planning, Manufacturing and racing ALL-TERRAIN Vehicles. The competition Goal include safety, good engineering and design. Each team goal is to design and build a prototype single seat, off-road recreational four wheel vehicle. It should be able to negotiate in all types of weather without any damage.







Departments



Frame & Ergonomics

A frame acts as the skeleton for the entire vehicle and is designed while taking care of ergonomics. It provides a rigid connection between the front and rear suspension, creates structural support for the other necessary systems and provides protection and comfort for the driver.

Suspension and Steering

Suspension acts as connecting medium between the sprung and unsprung mass of the ATV. It is crucil for the vehicles's stability.Steering system is a mechanical linkage which is helpful for a vehicle to follow the desired course.

Upsprung & brakes

Unsprung & brakes department primarily works on wheel assembly and brakes of vehicles and deals with tires,wheels, hubs, master cylinder, brakes callipers, pedals and rotars. Steering kunckle is also designed and fbricated with input from suspension & steering department.

Power Train

The Powertrain department forms the powerhouse of the vehicle. With Briggs and Stratton Engine as power source, a CVT is used as primary transmission component, and a Garbox (with differential) as a fixed, final reduction stage. Our main aim is to achieve balance between efficiency and Performance.
